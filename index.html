Rango

Director: Gore Verbinski

Genre: Contemporary Western

Released: 2011

Awards: Academy Award for Best Animated Feature
Fun Facts: "Rango" features a unique recording process where actors performed together on set instead of in isolation, enhancing their performances.

"Rango" follows the adventures of a chameleon who accidentally ends up in the lawless town of Dirt. Assuming the role of a sheriff, Rango must navigate the challenges of the Wild West, confront his own identity, and help the townsfolk save their community from a looming water crisis.
